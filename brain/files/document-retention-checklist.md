# Financial Document Retention Checklist

## Store weekly
- POS sales summary
- Deposit proof
- Vendor invoices
- Payroll/labor report
- Catering invoices and payments
- Refund/discount report
- Tax reserve record
- Royalty and marketing fund calculation

## Store monthly
- Bank statement
- P&L
- Balance sheet
- Sales tax filing proof
- Insurance/licensing updates
- Any equipment financing or lease documents

## Flo rule
Flo may summarize and flag missing documents, but should never fabricate financial records or treat estimates as final accounting.
