# Content Calendar + Campaign Rhythm

## Weekly baseline
- Monday: catering/conversion asset.
- Tuesday: raw build-in-public operator story.
- Wednesday: food proof or catering reel.
- Thursday: local community/event schedule.
- Friday: founder/business/franchise lesson.

## Approval flow
Idea -> draft -> asset -> approval -> scheduled -> posted -> measured.

## Flo intake prompts
- What happened?
- Why does it matter to customers/operators?
- Is there a photo/video?
- What CTA should be attached?
