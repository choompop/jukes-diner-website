# Weekly Finance Rhythm

## Daily close
- Record sales, deposits, refunds, discounts, cash variance, and weather/event notes.
- Flag any missing deposit or unexplained variance before the next service.

## Weekly review
1. Sales vs plan
2. Food cost and waste notes
3. Labor schedule vs sales volume
4. Cash on hand and upcoming bills
5. Catering leads and close rate
6. Royalties, marketing fund, tax reserve
7. One operating fix for next week

## Franchise principle
Successful franchise systems make financial review rhythmic, boring, and visible. Operators should not need to guess whether the week was healthy.
