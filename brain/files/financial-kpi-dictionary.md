# Financial KPI Dictionary

## Prime cost
Food cost percent + labor cost percent. This is the controllable core of restaurant economics.

## Four-wall operating profit
Sales minus unit-level operating costs before corporate overhead. Use it to compare units cleanly.

## Cash runway
Cash on hand divided by average weekly cash burn or obligations. Use before owner draws.

## Breakeven weekly sales
The weekly sales level needed to cover fixed and semi-fixed costs.

## Royalty reserve
Contractual franchisor royalty owed from gross sales.

## Marketing fund reserve
Required brand/marketing contribution owed from gross sales.
