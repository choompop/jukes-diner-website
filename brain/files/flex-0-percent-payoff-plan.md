## Pitfalls to Avoid

1. **Last-minute payment:** Processing can take 3-5 business days; don't wait until June 24
2. **Partial payment:** Some 0% offers revoke the rate if balance isn't $0 by expiry
3. **Autopay reliance:** Final payoff often requires manual intervention; verify it's set up correctly
4. **Grace period assumption:** Not all accounts honor end-of-month; some expire on the exact date
5. **Weekend/holiday timing:** June 25 falls on a Thursday; plan around any bank holidays that week
