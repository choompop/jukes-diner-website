📋 **Kitchen 7 (TPATCK) Shutdown Checklist Reminder**

Team — we've formalized the shutdown/housekeeping procedures for Kitchen 7 to reduce fire risk, maintain cleanliness standards, and keep our landlord relationship strong.

**Every time you finish service:**
✅ Vent hood fans OFF  
✅ Back light OFF  
✅ All burners/ovens OFF (visually confirm)  
✅ Stove/oven spills wiped down  
✅ Trash removed to dumpster (lids closed)  
✅ Gate secured  
✅ NO smoking/vaping indoors (lease violation)  

**Why this matters:**
- Leaving hood fans or burners on = fire hazard + insurance liability
- Spills/trash = pests, failed inspections, landlord issues
- Policy violations = eviction/fines risk

Full checklist posted in Kitchen 7 near the exit. Questions or equipment issues → flag in Slack or report via Flo.
