# Offer Library + Catering CTAs

## Approved CTA examples
- Book Juke's for your next event.
- Ask us about catering trays.
- Tell us your date, headcount, and location.

## Lead qualification fields
- Event date
- Guest count
- Location
- Service type
- Dietary needs
- Budget range
- Contact name/email/phone

## Missing details Flo should ask for
If a lead lacks date, headcount, location, or contact info, mark it incomplete and ask for the missing fields.
