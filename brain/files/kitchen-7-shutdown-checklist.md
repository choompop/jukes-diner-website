# Kitchen 7 (TPATCK) Shutdown & Housekeeping Checklist

**Location:** The Peach And The Coconut Kitchen, Kitchen 7  
**Purpose:** Prevent fire hazards, maintain cleanliness standards, and preserve landlord relationship  
**Owner:** Operations Lead  
**Updated:** 2026-05-08

---

## End-of-Service Shutdown (Every Use)

### Fire Safety & Equipment
- [ ] **Vent hood fans OFF** — Turn off all exhaust fans before leaving
- [ ] **Back light OFF** — Switch off overhead/back area lighting
- [ ] **All burners/ovens OFF** — Visually confirm all stove knobs in OFF position
- [ ] **Oven interiors checked** — No smoldering food, pans removed, doors closed

### Cleaning & Spills
- [ ] **Stove/oven spills wiped down** — Clean any grease, sauce, or food residue immediately
- [ ] **Cooking surfaces cleaned** — Wipe down prep areas, cutting boards stored
- [ ] **Floors swept** — No food debris left on kitchen floor
- [ ] **Trash removed** — All kitchen trash bagged and taken to dumpster

### Facility Rules
- [ ] **No smoking/vaping inside** — Kitchen and indoor spaces are 100% smoke-free
- [ ] **Gate secured** — Close and lock perimeter gate if applicable
- [ ] **Dumpster lids closed** — After trash disposal, close dumpster lids completely (pest/odor control)

---

## Weekly Deep Clean (Friday or as scheduled)
- [ ] Hood filters degreased
- [ ] Refrigerator/freezer interiors wiped
- [ ] Floor mopped with degreaser
- [ ] Shelving and dry storage organized

---

## Violation Risks
**Fire:** Leaving hood fans, burners, or ovens on creates fire hazard and insurance liability  
**Cleanliness:** Spills and trash attract pests, fail health inspections, and damage landlord relationship  
**Policy:** Smoking/vaping indoors violates lease terms and can trigger eviction or fines  

---

## Escalation
**Issue:** Damaged equipment, spill that can't be cleaned, or gate/lock problems  
**Action:** Report immediately to Operations Lead or via Flo with urgency flag  

---

**Printable version:** Post this checklist near the kitchen exit or hand-washing sink.
