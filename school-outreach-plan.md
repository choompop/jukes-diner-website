## OBJECTIVE

Build a pipeline of school catering and event bookings (K-12 and university) by targeting:
1. **University campus events** - High-traffic, high-volume opportunities (orientation, game days, student activities)
2. **High school staff appreciation** - Repeat annual events with strong budgets
3. **Fundraising partnerships** - PTA/booster clubs where Juke's can donate % of sales
4. **Private school events** - Affluent demographics, strong event budgets

---

## TARGET SEGMENTS

### 1. Universities (High-traffic events)

**Priority Targets:**
- **UT Austin** - Student union events, game days (football, basketball), orientation weeks, Greek life
- **Texas State University (San Marcos)** - Campus events, Greek life, spring/fall orientation
- **Austin Community College** - Multiple campuses, student life events, career fairs

**Key Contacts to Identify:**
- Student Activities Coordinator
- University Events Office
- Greek Life Advisor
- Athletics Catering Coordinator

**Value Proposition:**
- Self-contained setup (no kitchen needed)
- Fast service for high-volume crowds
- Affordable student pricing
- Social media promotion to student followers

---

### 2. High Schools (Staff Appreciation, Fundraisers)

**Priority Districts:**
- **Austin ISD** - Large district, multiple Teacher Appreciation Week events (typically May)
- **Round Rock ISD** - Suburban district, strong PTA funding
- **Eanes ISD (West Lake)** - Affluent area, generous staff appreciation budgets

**Private Schools:**
- St. Stephen's Episcopal School
- St. Andrew's Episcopal School
- Austin Waldorf School

**Key Contacts to Identify:**
- Principal / Assistant Principal
- PTA President
- Booster Club President (for fundraising events)
- Facilities/Events Coordinator

**Value Proposition:**
- Fundraising revenue share (suggest 15-20% of sales)
- Hassle-free catering for staff appreciation lunches
- Licensed, insured, health-permitted vendor
- Support local Austin small business

## RISK MITIGATION

**Potential Blockers:**
1. **School procurement policies** - Some districts require vendor registration/approval (can take 30-60 days)
   - **Mitigation:** Start with private schools and universities (less red tape), build references
2. **Health permit verification** - Schools may request proof of health permits, insurance
   - **Mitigation:** Have PDF copies ready to attach to first email
3. **Budget timing** - Schools plan events 60-90 days out
   - **Mitigation:** Pitch spring events now (March-May), summer camps in April, fall events in June
